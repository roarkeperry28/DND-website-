Major Locations

From storm-lashed monasteries to skyships, hellscapes, and spider-haunted caverns. Use this as a visual travel log and a quick reference before each session.

The Dawn of Ashes
Skyship • Transition to Tyranny of Dragons

The enchanted ship that carries the party from the burning of Greenest into the wider war. Its hull remembers every storm, and its crew has more secrets than the cargo hold.

The Scars of Avernus
First Layer of Hell • Future Arc

A wasteland of shattered fortresses, infernal war machines, and chains that vanish into the blood-red sky. Here, devils fight not just demons — but each other, for Zariel’s fading favor.

The Cocoon of Silent Webs
Underdark • Lolth’s Awakening

Far below the world, a god-spider dreams inside a shifting cocoon of silk and shadow. Every strand pulls at fate above — drow cities, surface wars, even the dragons of the world.

Dragon’s Rest, Stormwreck Isle
Prologue • Where it All Began

Salt-stung stone, dragon-blessed winds, and the first whispers of the Hollow Flame. The monastery’s quiet halls hide visions that won’t stop echoing.

The Well of Dragons
Tyranny of Dragons • Final Battle

A mass grave of dragons turned into a colossal altar. Here, cultists dare to tear open reality and crown Tiamat Queen of Dragons once more — unless your party breaks the ritual first.
