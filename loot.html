Relics & Rewards

Not just +1 swords. These are story-shaped treasures tied to dragons, devils, and the threads of fate around your party. Use them as inspiration for loot drops or long-term quest rewards.

Rare
Echo-Flute of the Hollow Star
Instrument (requires attunement by Lyrixia)

Silver flute inlaid with faint constellations. Once per long rest, Lyrixia can weave a performance that echoes across planes, granting allies advantage on a key encounter — or letting a secret message ride the song to a distant ear.

Found in a ruined dragon shrine, still humming with forgotten hymns.

Very Rare
Blisterbark, Cursed Infernal Musket
Weapon (attunement by Ghost Hands)

A talking musket forged in the Nine Hells, sarcastic and bloodthirsty. Deals extra necrotic or fire damage and can whisper secrets from souls it has slain — but it hungrily tempts its wielder toward darker choices.

Upgrades as Ghost Hands confronts her own death and the cult that caused it.

Rare
Emberblood Greataxe
Weapon (attunement by Teki)

A brutal axe whose blade drips molten light. When Teki rages, the weapon erupts in wild fire, sometimes bending reality around his swings — teleport sparks, sudden bursts of flame, or searing afterimages.

A relic “prophesied” by the Cult of the Hollow Flame, reclaimed for vengeance.

Rare
Halo of the Gentle Shield
Wondrous item (attunement by Goose Honk)

A soft golden halo that appears when Goose stands between an enemy and an ally. Grants powerful protective reactions and allows her to turn killing blows into unconsciousness instead — if the attacker can be redeemed.

Manifested during a moment when Goose chooses mercy over victory.

Very Rare
Cocoon-Thread Cloak
Cloak (attunement by Syla or Elara)

Woven from silvery strands stolen from Lolth’s evolving cocoon. Grants shadowstep through web-like cracks in reality, but occasional whispers urge the wearer to abandon friends for “greater hunts”.

Harvested in a desperate raid into the Underdark, beneath Lolth’s gaze.

Legendary
Void-Sigil Grimoire
Wondrous item (attunement by ZOX)

A living book whose pages are starless night. Grants access to reality-bending invocations, telepathy across planes, and glimpses of the moment Tiamat first opened her eyes — but pages sometimes write back.

The grimoire awakens fully after ZOX makes a choice that even their patron cannot easily predict.

Legendary
Shard of the Broken Chain
Campaign Relic (party attunement)

A fragment of the infernal bindings that once held Tiamat in Avernus. When carried by the whole party, it can weaken dragon cult rituals, disrupt devil contracts, and — at the very end — tip the scales in the final confrontation.

Assemble all fragments over the course of the campaign to unlock its true power.
